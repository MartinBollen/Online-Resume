Work Experience

David Lloyd Leisure (2001 - 2009)

I worked for David Lloyd Leisure from the age of 17, initially as a part time party co-ordinator at the Raynes Park site and eventually moved into several full time roles across multiple sites.

Part time roles included:

  • Childrens Party Co-ordinator

My first full time role with David Lloyd was as a maintenance operative, responsible for maintenance and reactive repair of the bowling lanes and the building.

After leaving David Lloyd in Raynes Park I held maintenance roles at the Brooklands and Hampton sites where I was charged with maintaining all elements of the sites including the swimming pools and spa facilities.

Maintaining and repairing the swimming pool and spa facilities was something I really enjoyed which led to me leaving David Lloyd and pursuing a career within the swimming pool indystry.

London Swimming Pool Company (2009 - 2015)

With my previous experience maintaining swimming pools and spa facilities I starte working for London Swimming Pools as a trainee and quickly established myself as a fully fledged engineer within 6 months.

As my time progressed with London Swimming Pools and I gained more experience and confidence and took on roles with more responsibilities, taking a senoir engineer role before being promoted to Field Service Manager.

In this role I was responsible for leading a team of engineers of varying skill and knowledge levels, carrying out site visits to advise potential new clients, and scheduling works and training new staff.
